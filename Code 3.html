Earthquakes

Earthquakes involve the powerful movement of rocks in the Earth’s crust. Scientists use the different speeds of seismic waves to locate the epicentre of earthquakes. Seismometers are used to measure the magnitude of earthquakes.

Precautions to take before an Earthquake

  • Learn about the earthquake hazard of the settlement you live in.
  • Earthquake-resistant buildings are way safer to live in.
  • You should inform your family, friends, and neighbors about what to do in case of an earthquake.
  • Test lying down, protect your head and hold onto something
  • If you’re at school, work, or any shopping area don’t take the elevator. Take the stairs.
